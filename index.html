Luca Bartolomei

I am currently a PhD candidate at Vision for Robotics Lab at ETH Zürich, Switzerland, since April 2018. My research focuses mostly on active perception, path planning, reinforcement learning and multi-robot coordination. Plus, my interests include visual-inertial odometry and camera-based SLAM.

I finished my Master in Robotics, System and Control in 2018 always at ETH Zürich, while I got a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano (PoliMi) in 2015.

In between the Bachelor and the Master studies, I worked as Software Engineer Inter at Schindler Aufzüge AG.
